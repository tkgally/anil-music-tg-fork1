Focus pulse (experimental)
Steady 16 Hz amplitude modulation on the pad+bass bed, modeled on Brain.fm's approach; evidence is mixed and strongest for listeners with attention difficulties. Try it — some people love it, some can't stand it. It ramps in over 30 s; the rate and phase never change (predictable modulation entrains, irregular modulation distracts).
